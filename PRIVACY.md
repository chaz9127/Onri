# Privacy Policy for Onri

_Effective date: April 9, 2026_

---

## Overview

Onri is a browser extension that displays an animated dinosaur sprite on your browser window. This policy describes what data the extension does and does not collect.

---

## Data Collection

Onri does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data. The extension has no servers, makes no network requests, and sends no information anywhere.

---

## Local Storage

The extension stores one value locally on your device — your show/hide preference for the sprite. This value never leaves your browser and is not accessible to any third party.

---

## Permissions

- **storage** — Used solely to remember whether you have hidden or shown the sprite between sessions.
- **activeTab** — Used solely to deliver your show/hide toggle to the current tab. No tab content is read.
- **Host permissions** — The sprite overlay is rendered on pages you visit. The extension does not read, collect, or interact with any content on those pages.

---

## Third Parties

Onri does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

---

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published with a new effective date.

---

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, you can open an issue at the extension's repository.
